६४ कला Competition at a glance

It is a unique and highly engaging Kids Contest based on our Indian Arts and Culture for Kids between 2 to 12 years and their Parents. Beyond just contest, it is a crafted Art Journey to let your kids Know, Explore, Perform and Showcase all 64 Indian Arts step by step with one art per month in a very engaging and competitive way.

January -

Geet Vidya: Art of singing

Sing along to a simple, familiar rhyme or lullaby with a parent

February -

Chitra Karma: Art of painting

Explore colors by finger painting or using large brushes to create a simple image.

March -

Kavyakarnapatha: Art of reciting poetry

Recite a short, simple rhyme or verse with the help of a parent, focusing on rhythm.

April -

Nritya Vidya: Art of dancing

Dance to a simple, upbeat song with basic movements like clapping, twirling, and hopping.

May -

Natya Vidya: Art of theatrics

Act out a simple scene from a favorite story, using basic expressions and gestures.

June -

Nritya Yoga: Art of dancing

Follow along with simple dance moves, focusing on balance and coordination.

July -

Nepathya Yoga: Art of wearing costumes

Dress up in simple costumes related to a story or theme, with help from a parent

August -

Vinoda: Art of story-telling

Retell a simple, familiar story with the help of pictures or puppets, focusing on key events.

September -

Vadya Vidya: Art of playing musical instruments

Explore sounds by playing simple percussion instruments like tambourines or maracas.

October -

Malya Grathana Vikalpa: Art of designing different kinds of garlands

Create a simple garland using large beads or paper flowers, with help from a parent

November -

Chitrasena: Art of drawing in colors

Draw simple shapes and fill them with bright colors, focusing on large, easy-to-hold crayons.

December -

Alekhya Vidya: Art of painting

Use large brushes and bright colors to create simple, abstract paintings.
