What is the ६४ कला Competition?

 
The ६४ कला are a diverse collection of traditional Indian art forms, skills, and disciplines, each representing a unique facet of human creativity and expression. Rooted in ancient Indian wisdom, these Kalas were designed to nurture a person’s holistic development—encompassing physical, intellectual, emotional, and spiritual growth. The concept of the ६४ कला was first introduced in classical Indian literature, with references in texts such as the Natyashastra, and various Puranas, symbolizing a well-rounded and refined education system that went far beyond mere academics.

Unleash your creativity by diving into the enchanting world of garland-making! Whether it’s flowers, beads, or fabrics, let their imagination run wild and create something truly special!

Challenge Highlights:

  • Theme: Garlands Inspired by Nature and Culture
  • Materials: Flowers, leaves, beads, fabric, or any creative elements
  • Duration: Create your garland over the month and submit by 13th October.
