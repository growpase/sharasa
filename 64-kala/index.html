What is the ६४ कला Competition?

The ६४ कला Competition is an engaging and educational platform designed for children age 2 to 12, encouraging them to explore and showcase traditional Indian art forms. Based on the ancient concept of the "६४ कला," or "64 Arts," this competition introduces children to a variety of creative, cultural, and performance arts, from singing, dancing, and painting to crafts, storytelling, and more. Each month, a new art form challenge is presented, tailored to specific age groups, giving children a chance to explore different skills and talents.

Chitrasena- Art of Drawing in Colors

Dive into the world of India’s traditional 64 Kalas with this vibrant daily drawing challenge! We invite artists of all ages to explore a new art theme each day, transforming ancient skills into unique, colorful creations. From storytelling to design, let your creativity flow and bring each Kala to life in your own style!

Challenge Highlights:

  • Theme: Drawing inspired by traditional Kalas of Indian art and culture
  • Materials: Any drawing medium—pencils, markers, paints, or digital tools
  • Duration: Create your drawing over the month and submit by 10th November.
