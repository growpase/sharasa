What is the ६४ कला Contest?

६४ कला is a unique Indian Art Education platform by ShaRaSa for Child between 2 to 12 years - to introduce them with 64 Indian Arts with highly engaging and competitive way. This engaging Art Journey to will unleash all 64 Indian Arts within your child step by step with focussing only one art per month Competitive and Rewarding structure of this Art journey is uniquely designed to build awareness, interest and exposure of our valuable Indian Arts.
  • Mode : Online Kids Contest
  • Age Group : 2 to 12 Years
  • Monthly Contest : One Art per Month
  • Art Challenge : Perform and Submit from Home
This is Contest to perform from your home!
  • Judges : Experts from the fields
  • Selection : Winners of the Month

Chitrasena- Art of Drawing in Colors
